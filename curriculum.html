Disability Studies Curriculum

An interactive syllabus for learning about disability studies and accessibility.

Module 1: Introduction to Disability Studies

This module covers the fundamental concepts of disability studies, including:

  • Historical perspectives on disability
  • Social vs. medical models of disability
  • Intersectionality and disability

Module 2: Accessibility in the Digital Age

Explore the challenges and opportunities for accessibility in technology:

  • Web accessibility standards (WCAG)
  • Assistive technologies and their impact
  • Universal Design principles

Module 3: Legal and Ethical Considerations

Understand the legal landscape and ethical implications of accessibility:

  • Disability rights legislation (e.g., ADA, Section 508)
  • Ethical considerations in accessible design
  • Case studies in accessibility law
