Spoon Theory

Spoon Theory is a metaphor used to explain the limited energy available to those with chronic illnesses or disabilities. Key points:

  • Spoons represent units of energy
  • Each task costs a certain number of spoons
  • People have a limited number of spoons per day
  • Managing spoons helps prioritize tasks and conserve energy
  • Spoon count can vary day-to-day based on health

This tool helps you manage your daily "spoons" and prioritize tasks accordingly.

Typical range: 0-50 spoons. Adjust as needed for your personal scale.
